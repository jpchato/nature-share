Guidelines

  • Share organisms you encounter outdoors; keep uploads relevant and respectful.
  • Respect wildlife and habitats; avoid disturbance and follow local laws.
  • Safety first. Foraging or tasting anything you find is at your own risk.
  • Edibility claims are not verified. Always consult multiple trusted sources before consuming any wild food.
